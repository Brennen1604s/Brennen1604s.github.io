Custom Websites

Inquiring about a custom website

Inquiring about a custom website is simple! Message us at KreateYourWebsite@gmail.com. In your email, please give a description of the website you wish for us to create custom for you. Please include information such as your company name, and a basic idea of what you want your site to look like. When you send us your email with this information, we will replay saying whether or not we are willing to create it for you. Pricing for custom websites varies on how much time it will take to be complete. Once we complete your website, we will send you the download folder containing all of the files that make up your website via email.

What should be included in your email

  • Your company name
  • A description of your company to include in the website
  • Your company contact information
  • What pages you want on your navigation bar (ex. Home, Blog, Store)
  • What information you want to be included on each page (if you wish to have multiple pages in your navigation bar)
  • Your company colors (To be included in webpage design)
  • Where you would like pictures (If you want any pictures)

Additional Information

Along with the information that needs to be included in your email, we are going to need your help with the creation of this website. Your job is to hold all of the pictures for your website on your computer. For security purposes, we do not want to install images through email. We will create the places for the images on the website. All you have to do is add them to the page. Instructions on how to do this will be included in the downloadable folder.
